The Problem We're Solving

In today's competitive landscape, conducting online exams can be a daunting task for companies and organizations. Manually visiting colleges, conducting interviews, and assessing answers is time-consuming, effort-intensive, and costly. This often leads to deserving students from low-grade colleges being overlooked, resulting in a mismatch between talent and job opportunities.

Our Solution

Examen is an innovative online examination system that streamlines the entire process, making it easier for companies to find the best candidates and for students to get exposed to their desired job profiles. Our platform allows companies to host exams, automatically checks answers, and generates instant results, reducing paperwork and anxiety for students.

Our Objectives

  • Provide an efficient online examination system that reduces the manual effort of assessing answers
  • Offer instant results, reducing anxiety for students
  • Create various reports and graphs for evaluation purposes
  • Facilitate a seamless connection between students and companies, promoting a more inclusive and merit-based hiring process

Our Vision

At Examen, we envision a future where every student has an equal opportunity to showcase their skills, regardless of their college or background. We strive to create a platform that is accessible, reliable, and efficient, ultimately leading to a more equitable and talent-driven job market.
